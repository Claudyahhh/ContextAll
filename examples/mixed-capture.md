# Fictional mixed capture

I realized Project Atlas should not begin as a large community. The first useful artifact is probably a weekly decision log. I do not yet know whether people will pay for it.

Yesterday I spoke with Mira. She thought the difficult part was not writing the log but returning to it when making a decision. I disagreed at first, then noticed I rarely revisit my own notes.

I like interfaces that feel warm rather than clinical. Use soft pink as the main surface, pale yellow as support, and deep blue only for structure and emphasis.
